about.

located in Denver, CO.

I have been learning about programming for the better part of 4 years. In 2018, I attended
and graduated from a coding boot camp at the University of Denver where I learned about
HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, React, and much more. I came to a
realization that while the boot camp laid a sturdy foundation for my programming knowledge,
I needed to further my education. I chose to enroll at Regis University where I earned my
Bachelor of Science in Computer Science in 2022. Through my education, I've learned the
importance of fully functional programs that also maintain a high standard of design to
ensure optimal UI/UX.
